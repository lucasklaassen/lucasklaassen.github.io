LMK QBO Integration

2026-06-19

Last updated: June 19, 2026

App

LMK QBO Integration — an internal tool for LMK Management Inc.

Scope

This integration is for single-user, internal use only. It is not offered as a public product and is not available to third parties.

Access

The integration connects to QuickBooks Online through Intuit’s official API. Authentication is handled via OAuth and runs locally on the developer’s machine.

Your Data

Data accessed from QuickBooks Online is used solely for internal bookkeeping and reporting by LMK Management Inc. Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Credentials

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the developer’s machine (in a .env file). They are not stored on a public server or in any cloud database.

Disconnect

You can revoke access at any time via Intuit → Settings → Connected Apps. See the disconnect notice for details.
