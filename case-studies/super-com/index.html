How Super.com Scaled an AI-First Engineering Culture in 5 Weeks

Bridging the gap between 80+ engineers and their tools to drive a 244% increase in agentic workflow adoption.

Client

Super.com

Industry

Fintech / Travel

Services

  • AI Strategy & Governance
  • RAG & Agentic Workflows
  • AI-Native Engineering Operations
  • Technical Hiring

The Challenge

Scaling "Pockets of Excellence" Super.com had smart engineers and robust tools, but adoption was uneven. While "power users" leveraged AI for high-level planning and execution, other teams relied solely on basic tab completion. This competency gap created a massive opportunity for education, but the organization lacked a unified engine to drive it.

The Solution

The AI Enablement Sprint We didn't need to rebuild the stack; we needed to distribute the best practices that already existed in silos. We executed a 5-week "Enablement Sprint" focused on education, tooling awareness, and process standardization.
  • "Keeping Agents On a Leash": We shifted the mental model from "Author" to "Architect & Reviewer". We taught engineers to avoid "Lazy Velocity" by applying a strict workflow: providing the AI with clear intent and constraints (the leash) while retaining full responsibility for verification and system design.
  • 18 Mob Programming Sessions: We moved beyond theory, gathering teams to fix real bugs live. These sessions showcased how to “Keep Agents On A Leash” and utilize MCP integrations (Datadog, Jira) to give the Agent context.
  • Rule Lifecycle Governance: We empowered all teams with a clear protocol for when to create, update, and promote rules. This transformed "hidden" personal rules into shared global assets strengthened by an AI Guild review board.
  • Technical Hiring: Established the technical standards and evaluation criteria for the permanent AI Lead role.

The Results

  • 244% Increase in Adoption: Average total usage of the AI Coding Agent increased by 244%, proving a massive shift from "Tab Complete" to "Agentic Workflow".
  • Standardized Governance: Established the "Rule Lifecycle" process, empowering the AI Guild to effectively govern and share context across teams.
  • Unlocked Tooling ROI: Increased adoption of MCP & DevEx tools, ensuring engineers are finally utilizing the robust infrastructure the platform team has already built.
  • Secured the AI Lead: Successfully identified and vetted the permanent AI Lead, ensuring the strategy has a long-term owner.
